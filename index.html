Wheels to Wealth
20 Feb, 2025

The metaphor “don't reinvent the wheel” is commonly used to save us both time and redundant efforts. What Nikola Knows however is that the wheel has in fact been reinvented countless times throughout history. The fact that some vehicles can function with one, while others require dozens also highlights that there is no "one size fits all" methodology when it comes to getting your project rolling (see what we did there?) nor one that guarantees you won't hit some road bumps along the way. We know that sometimes you have to kick the tires to find the right vehicle that will get you where you need to go, and we designed our own roadmap to wealth that we're confident will reveal the missing pieces in your success plan like no other. We also like the idea of hiding digital wheels that you can collect as apart of our loyalty program. The more you interact, share, and keep coming back; the more wheels you can acquire and exchange for actual services, content and even sweet merch (coming soon!)
